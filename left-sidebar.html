Story

The Story of a child which wanted to become a pirate

Once apon a time, a boy called monkey d. luffy was friends with a pirate crew called the red haired pirates. He looked up to them, he wanted to become like them, but the one he looked up most was the captain, "red haired" shanks. Luffy got his rubber powers by eating the gomu gomu fruit by accident. After a while he challenged the mountain pirates where he got beat up.
Shanks saw what was happening and tried to save him, but the captain of the mountain pirates threw luffy into the water with him as devil fruit users cannot swim. Shanks jumped in to save Luffy, but a giant fish came by and chomped Shanks' arm before he scared him away. The mountain pirates had all died from the giant fish and the red hair pirates. Shanks gave Luffy his favorite hat and sailed away.
A few months later, Luffy became friends with 2 other kids, sabo and ace. They were basically brothers and wanted to become pirates together as even before they found out that sabo was actually a noble and his parents took him back from the trashland and back to his old house and met with his "adopted" brother. He soon got out and went on for sail when Luffy and Ace were finding him, he sent them a letter telling them that he has to go and do his own adventure. Eventually some people shot him down and he almost died until Luffy's father which was the most dangerous pirate saved him, but he forgot everything after he woke up.
After around a year of training, Luffy and Ace got over the "death" of Sabo it and went on their own adventures without knowing that he was alive.
